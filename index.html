About Hariprasad Kidney Centre

Established with a vision to provide exceptional kidney care, Hariprasad Kidney Centre has been serving patients for over 15 years. Our state-of-the-art facility combines advanced medical technology with personalized care to deliver the best possible outcomes for our patients.

We specialize in comprehensive nephrology services, including prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of kidney diseases. Our team of experienced nephrologists and healthcare professionals are committed to improving the quality of life for patients with kidney conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Early signs of kidney disease include changes in urination patterns, swelling in legs or ankles, fatigue, nausea, and high blood pressure. If you experience any of these symptoms, it's important to consult with a nephrologist for proper evaluation.

The frequency of kidney function tests depends on your risk factors and current health status. Generally, adults should have basic kidney function screening annually, while those with diabetes, high blood pressure, or family history of kidney disease may need more frequent testing.

Your first dialysis session will include a comprehensive assessment, explanation of the procedure, and careful monitoring. Our team will ensure you're comfortable and answer any questions. The session typically lasts 3-4 hours, and you'll be monitored throughout the process.

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency kidney care services. Our emergency team is equipped to handle acute kidney conditions, dialysis complications, and other urgent nephrology issues. Contact us immediately if you experience severe symptoms.

A kidney-friendly diet typically includes limiting sodium, phosphorus, and potassium intake while ensuring adequate protein. Our nutritionists work with patients to create personalized meal plans. It's important to follow specific dietary guidelines based on your individual condition and stage of kidney disease.
